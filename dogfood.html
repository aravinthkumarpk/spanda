Try Spanda

A 5-minute first run. No prior knowledge needed — just follow the steps and tick them off. ← roadmap

Spanda is a simple board for your real tasks. Both you and AI agents work the same board. Everything you see is your live task list (the “beads” system) — Spanda is just a clean window onto it.

Before you start — is the latest version live? Open the app and check the top bar shows Projects · Board · Today tabs. If you don’t see a Board tab, the server is running an old build — ask for a redeploy first, or the steps below won’t match.

Open it

0Go to https://spanda.onyourdevice.ai

Sign in if it asks (Cloudflare Access — your normal login). It opens straight onto the Board.

You should see four columns left-to-right: To do · Doing · Review · Done, with your real tasks shown as cards.

The Board — your tasks at a glance

1Read a card. Each card shows the task title, a small coloured state tag, and a bucket chip (do / coordinate / followup).

Look for a small orange “stale 8d” tag on cards that haven’t moved in a while.

2Filter by bucket. Above the columns, click a chip like Do or Coordinate.

You should see the board narrow to just that bucket. Click All to reset.

3Edit a task. On any card, click the little pencil. Change the title, then press Update.

You should see a “Task updated” message and the card show the new title. Tip: for a do task, the form won’t let you save without acceptance criteria — that’s on purpose.

Projects — the bird’s-eye view

4Click the Projects tab in the top bar.

You should see your projects as cards — e.g. Launch Agent Marketplace, AI Transformation, Personal & Family — each with a health badge (Moving / Stalled / Blocked / Done) and a count of tasks under it.

Today — your daily review

5Click Today (top bar) or go to /today.

You should see today’s written review. On lines your daily marks as actionable, a small green “make it work” button appears — click it to turn that line into a real task (a form opens pre-filled; review and confirm). If you see no buttons yet, your daily pipeline hasn’t tagged any lines — that’s expected.

Add a task from scratch

6Create one. Hit / or the + Add button. Type a title. If it’s a do task, fill the Acceptance criteria too. Press Done.

You should see the new task land in the To do column.

What to jot down as you go

  • Did each screen load, and show your real tasks (not an empty board)?
  • Anything confusing, mislabeled, or in the wrong column?
  • Did edit / add / promote actually save (and survive a refresh)?
  • Did any project show the wrong health, or tasks under the wrong project?
  • Anything slow, broken, or ugly.

That’s the whole tour. The four things to trust: the Board reflects your real tasks, edit/add/promote write straight to the task system (one source of truth), Projects rolls them up, and Today turns the daily into real work.
